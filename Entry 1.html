How strange it is to be anything at all




Does it ever strike you strange that we're even here? What are we doing and where are we going? We are on a rock hurtling in the vast emptiness of space, nothing but small specs in the grand scheme of things. I'm having oatmeal while I write this. I wonder who stood a thousand years ago on the spot I am on now. Everything feels bigger, older, and wiser than I am. Estranging but beautiful.


What do you think?
